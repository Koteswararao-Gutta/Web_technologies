Blood Pressure Machine

This machine has an adjustable large cuff that fits most adult arms. Reviews note it is lightweight and easy to use. However, some reviews say it could be more accurate.

Diabetes Testing Machine

A blood glucose meter, also known as a glucometer, is a small, portable device that measures the amount of glucose in the blood. People with diabetes often use a blood glucose meter to help them manage their condition.

Pregnancy Testing Kit

The pregnancy test kit detects the pregnancy hormone, human chorionic gonadotrophin (hCG), in your urine or serum. The test can be taken on the first day of your missing period. If you test too early then the hCG level in your urine may be too low to detect and could lead to a false negative.

Forehead Thermometer

This thermometer is easy to use and has an ultra-sensitive infrared sensor. Reviews note that it has a memory feature and is designed for accuracy. However, reviews also note that it requires two AAA batteries and may not always give an accurate reading if not held in the correct position.

Weight Checking Machine

This digital scale has four high-precision sensors that deliver trustworthy results with a 0.2 lb accuracy resolution. It has a large, sharp LCD display and seamless unit conversion with a single button. It also has an app that can measure thirteen different body metrics.
